## Scenario Assumptions

### Base case — 20% YoY growth
Datadog delivered 28% in FY2025. We model 20% as a conservative forward
estimate, consistent with natural deceleration after a high-growth year
and with analyst consensus for FY2026.

### EMEA Expansion — +8% ARR uplift
- Ramp: linear from 0% in month 1 to +8% by month 18
- New market NRR: starts at 95%, reaches 108% by month 18
- Investment: ~$80M (S&M headcount + regional infrastructure)
- Early churn drivers: GDPR compliance overhead, limited local
  customer success coverage, longer enterprise sales cycles

### Aggressive (EMEA + APAC) — +18% ARR uplift
- Ramp: 0% to +18% over 24 months
- New market NRR: 92% in year 1 (split GTM focus reduces
  both acquisition quality and retention)
- Investment: ~$180M
- Datadog FCF of $915M (FY2025) confirms this spend level
  is sustainable without impairing cash generation
